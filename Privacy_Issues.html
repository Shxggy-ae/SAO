Privacy Issues Online

Not everything you see online is true. Your personal information is constantly at risk from data breaches, tracking, and misuse. Learn how to protect yourself and understand the dangers.

Why It's Dangerous

Privacy violations can lead to identity theft, financial fraud, manipulation, and loss of personal freedom. Your data is often collected without your knowledge and sold to third parties for profit.

Data Breaches

Hackers steal personal information from companies, exposing emails, passwords, and credit card details.

Constant Tracking

Websites and apps track your location, browsing habits, and behavior to build profiles about you.

Data Selling

Companies sell your personal data to advertisers and marketers without clear consent.


How to Protect Yourself

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique, complex passwords for each account. Use a password manager to keep them safe.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra security layer to your accounts so hackers can't access them with just your password.

Check Privacy Settings

Review and limit what information social media and apps can access about you.

Block Tracking

Use privacy browser extensions and disable location tracking when not needed.

Tip: If you're unsure whether a site is safe, don't enter your personal information. When in doubt, it's better to skip it.
